FOCUS ON WHAT MATTERS

Focus generally refers to the ability to concentrate attention and mental effort on a particular task or activity. It involves directing your cognitive resources toward a specific goal or objective while filtering out distractions. When someone is focused, they are fully engaged and immersed in what they are doing, which often leads to increased productivity and better performance.
